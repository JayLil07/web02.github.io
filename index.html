Who We Are

Puppy Spa & Play provides premium grooming and daycare services for your beloved pets. Located in the heart of Ottawa, just minutes away from the ByWard Market, our little but charming facility has been a fan favorite for pet owners since 1983. Featuring expert grooming sessions and engaging playtime, we aim to keep your pets looking their best and feeling happy. Look no further but the very best for your furry friend!

Learn More About Us

What We Do

Here at Puppy Spa & Play, we offer joint grooming and daycare services for those who cannot always take care for their pet in the way they need, whether that be because of age, physical disability, a busy schedule, or a lack of resources. The staff at Puppy Spa & Play are determined to give each pet the love they deserve, and do not discriminate against breed, age, or background. Our clients can rely on us for reliable pet care during the workday and for their pets to leave with softer fur than they came in with!
